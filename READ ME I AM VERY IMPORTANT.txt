-------------------------
INSTALLATION INSTRUCTIONS
-------------------------

This mod requires the decompiled version of Sonic Mania. This mod is not designed for the Origin version of the game.
The Plus DLC is *not* required to play this mod.
 
If a cutscene hangs, just press Enter and it will skip.
